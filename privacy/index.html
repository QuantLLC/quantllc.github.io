Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026 · Quant LLC · England, United Kingdom

Quant LLC (“Quant”, “we”, “us”) operates the Quant website and related products. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Account details you provide (email, display name).
  • Authentication metadata (email verification status, sign-in times).
  • Settings you choose (theme, currency, risk profile, preferences).
  • Download / product interaction events you trigger while signed in.
  • Basic technical data needed to run the site (browser, device type).

How we use it

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To personalise your dashboard and preferences.
  • To support you when you contact us.
  • To improve product reliability and prevent abuse.

Storage

Account data is stored using Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Passwords are hashed by Firebase and are never stored by us in plain text.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it with infrastructure providers required to run the service (e.g. Firebase / Google Cloud), or when required by law.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data by emailing quantllcsupport@proton.me, or by using Delete account in Settings.
