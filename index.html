WHAT IS QUANT

Your money, managed by a machine that never sleeps.

Deposit funds and Quant does the rest. It continuously researches the market, reads the news, and buys and sells on your behalf — aiming to hand you back your capital plus passive earnings.

01

Runs on your own server

Quant runs locally on a server you control — flashed from an ISO you download. Your keys, your machine, your data.

02

Research-driven trading

Using Yahoo Finance plus news from many sources, Quant gathers as much signal as possible to judge whether a holding is set to climb or crash.

03

Decisive risk control

If a crash is likely it pulls out immediately; if a rebound is expected it holds. Every decision is made to protect and grow your balance.

04

Monitor from your phone

The Android app lets you view status anywhere. Control stays physical: pair a Bluetooth keyboard and use your phone as a mouse and monitor.
